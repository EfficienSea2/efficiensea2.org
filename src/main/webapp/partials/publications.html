The list below is an ongoing list of free-to-view publications produced in the EfficienSea 2 project. You can also see EfficienSea 2 presentations made at conferences and other promotional events.

Project briefs and presentations

The leaflet and presentations communicate the purpose, scope and results of the EfficienSea2 project at a general level – in shorter and longer versions. The leaflet is designed for and disseminated to a broad audience. The presentations has been used internally in the project as well as externally at conferences and seminars in the maritime world.

Report on relevant ongoing external projects and test beds

This report sets out the strategy for carrying out the work of identifying the projects, test beds and organizations relevant to E2.
The report summarizes more detailed information collected in the project. The methods of providing access to the information for EfficienSea2 partners are also discussed. The report is part of the overall aim of ensuring liaison and coordination with other relevant projects, test beds and policies.

Report on ongoing standardisation work relevant to the project

This report sets out the strategy for carrying out the task of identifying ongoing standardisation work, the organizations involved with standards relevant to the project and the main topics of interests. The methods of providing access to the information for Efficiency2 partners are also discussed. The report is part of the overall aim of ensuring coordination standardisation of solutions developed in EfficienSea2.

Analysis report on maritime communication and infrastructure

This report describes the needs related to infrastructure functions related to the ‘Maritime Cloud’ to support effective digital interactions in the maritime domain. The source of identified needs are use cases collected within the project as well as external projects.
